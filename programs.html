Volunteerism: A Step-by-Step Journey Program

- A Lamsat Ensan initiative that aims to instill the values of volunteering into the younger members of society. Under the program, adult volunteers will be matched will be matched with younger children (6-12 years old) who will train them to become the active volunteers of tomorrow through various interactive events. Through the program, the children will experience the joy of giving back to the community. This intergenerational program will incentive them to become active teens in tomorrow’s society. The program involves several events, which are announced periodically.
