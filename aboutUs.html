Who Are We?

We are a passionate youth group aspiring to strengthen communal ties between members of society through promoting the spirit of cooperation and empathy and to develop the role of volunteerism in creating societal solidarity. Our group is mobilized by the drive to show that there’s still hope for those desperately needing it through human compassion.

Our Philosophy

Our world concurrently faces many eminent threats on most fronts: environmentally, socially, and economically. From problems of poverty and hunger to those of sustainability and climate change, the world needs the active involvement of its youth to begin to fathom tackling such issues. The youth population is at least 40% of the world’s global population. Such a large percentage means that any contribution made by every individual from this group can lead to significant changes in the big picture. Our philosophy is that if we galvanize the youth in our communities to offer small contributions, whether in donations, volunteering involvement, or other forms of support, the amalgamation of those contributions, if directed to common goals, can lead to large-scale improvements in society.
