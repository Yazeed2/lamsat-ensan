Our Mission

  • To aggregate our youth community’s contributions to uplift and support members of our society who are disadvantaged, whether in living conditions or any other aspect of quality of life.

  • To inspire activity and involvement through programs that target various sectors of society.

  • To emphasize the role of volunteerism in creating a community spirit and unifying various members of society.

  • To provide an outlet for society’s youth to develop their interpersonal skills while nurturing a sense of empathy.

  • To play a role in tackling our world’s most pressing issues, such as economic inequality, poverty, and sustainability, as well as spreading sufficient awareness of them.
